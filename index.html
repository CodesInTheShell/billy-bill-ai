Billy Bill AI

An AI-assisted, community-sourced way to build better bills.

Billy Bill AI helps people draft legislation together. AI turns ideas into structured bill language, while the community reviews, suggests changes, and drives each proposal toward real-world impact.

Proof of concept only: this is a social testing website/app for the idea that AI and the community can work together to craft better bills — bills by the people, for the people.

What is a bill?

A bill is a proposal or draft of a law introduced in a legislature. It is law in the making, moving through readings, debates, and committee reviews before it can become law.

AI-powered drafting

Describe a problem in plain language and let AI generate a clear first draft with sections, policy goals, and implementation details to accelerate legislative work.

Community-sourced ideas

Citizens, advocates, and experts can suggest edits, submit supporting arguments, and vote on priorities so bills reflect real needs from the ground up.
