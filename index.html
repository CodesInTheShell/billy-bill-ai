Billy Bill AI

An AI-assisted, community-sourced way to build better bills.

Billy Bill AI helps people draft legislation together. AI turns ideas into structured bill language, while the community reviews, suggests changes, and drives each proposal toward real-world impact.

Proof of concept only: this is a social testing website/app for the idea that AI and the community can work together to craft better bills — bills by the people, for the people.

What is a bill?

A bill is a draft of a proposed law introduced for review, debate, and approval.

AI-powered drafting

Describe a problem in plain language and AI can produce a first legal draft quickly.

About Billy Bill AI

Billy Bill AI is a proof-of-concept civic platform where people can draft policy ideas and review AI-assisted bill text in one place.

The goal is to make legislative thinking easier to understand, easier to discuss, and more community-driven through transparent bill summaries and collaborative iteration.
